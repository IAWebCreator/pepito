HOW TO BUY

Get a Wallet

Download a reputable Ethereum wallet like MetaMask or Trust Wallet.

Acquire ETH

Purchase Ethereum (ETH) from a cryptocurrency exchange like Coinbase or Binance.

Connect to a DEX

Connect your wallet to a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap or SushiSwap.

Swap ETH for PTO

Use the DEX's swap feature to exchange your ETH for PEPITO (PTO) tokens.
